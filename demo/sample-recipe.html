Lemon Garlic Chicken

This local sample page includes JSON-LD recipe data. To test with the extension, serve this folder over HTTP, for example with: python3 -m http.server 8080

  • 500 g chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 cup rice, cooked
  • 1 bunch parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes, optional
